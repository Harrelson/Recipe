    Preparation

    Step 1: Marinade your Chicken

    1. Chop the Chicken in pieces (size to your liking), put it in a bowl.
    2. Add Soy Sauce, 1 tbsp. of Mustard, Thyme, Curry Powder and about 1 tbsp. of Flower
    3. Mix everything, put aside for about 30 Mins so the Marinade can take effect.

    Step 2: Cook the Chicken

    Let the Pan heat up (just don't trigger the Fire Alarm), add Olive Oil, sear the Chicken so it gets nice and crusty on all sides. Depending on your dish size, you might want to repeat this with smaller portions.

    Step 3: Prepare the Sauce

    1. After you've cooked the chicken, use the same pan and heat it up with some Olive Oil and add the Onions - simmer a bit.
    2. Chop the Bell Peppers and add to the Onions. Sear with high heat for about a Minute, then deglaze with some White Wine. Lower Heat.
    3. Add Curry Powder, little bit of Flower, and 1-2 Tbsp. of Mango Chutney.
    4. Add Bouillon (about 1-2 dl depending on how much sauce you want).
    5. After letting the Sauce simmer for a bit, add some Cream. Then let it simmer until the sauce thickens.
